## What is Pactory?

Pactory is a platform that helps you monetize your AI agents. Whether you've built your agent using no-code tools, LangChain, or custom code, Pactory provides the infrastructure to connect, monetize, and share your agents with the world.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Sign up for a Pactory account to access the platform
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Agent">
    Connect your existing AI agent through our supported integrations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Payments">
    Connect your Stripe account and configure your pricing model
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share & Earn">
    Share your agent's link and start earning from user interactions
  </Step>
</Steps>
